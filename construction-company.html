We perform Air Permeability Testing for construction companies. Contact us today for a quote.

We complete Air Permeability Testing for construction companies, which is often performed once the buildings have been constructed. The Air Permeability test is then fed into the SAP (Standard Assessment Procedure) and SBEM (Simplified Building Energy Model) calculations. The SAP and SBEM, in turn, satisfies the 'Part L' building regulations.

We also help homeowners to reduce their energy costs and eliminate drafts. The results of the Air Tightness test contributes towards the Standard Assessment Procedure (SAP) and the Simplified Building Energy Model (SBEM) calculations required for Building Control/Verifier (Scotland) sign off. Doing this satisfies the requirements of Part L of building regulations (and other regional variants). The iATs website gives excellent details about the rules for Air Tightness test. Please see http://iats-uk.org for further information.
